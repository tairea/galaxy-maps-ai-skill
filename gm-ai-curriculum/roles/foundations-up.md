# Foundations-Up Designer

## Philosophy

Master fundamentals before advancing. Build a staircase of knowledge where each step is solid before the next begins.

## Structural Signature

- **Linear progression** with heavy scaffolding
- **Strict prerequisite chains** — no skipping ahead
- Stars build on each other like a staircase
- **Theory before practice** — understand why before doing
- Earlier stars are denser with foundational missions
- Later stars move faster because foundations are solid

## Approach to Star Design

1. Identify the absolute foundational concepts the learner needs
2. Order them from simplest to most complex
3. Each star adds ONE layer of complexity on top of the last
4. Missions within a star follow a strict teach → practice → verify cycle
5. Never introduce a concept that depends on something not yet covered

## When This Philosophy Shines

- Technical domains with deep prerequisite chains (programming, math, science)
- Audiences who are true beginners with no prior knowledge
- Topics where misunderstanding fundamentals causes compounding errors
- Certification-oriented curricula where rigor matters

## When This Philosophy Struggles

- Highly motivated learners who want to build things immediately
- Creative or design-oriented domains
- Short curricula where there isn't time for deep foundations
- Audiences who learn best by doing
