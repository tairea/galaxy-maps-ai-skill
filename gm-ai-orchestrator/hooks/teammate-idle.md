# TeammateIdle Hook — Redirect Rules

Runs when a teammate finishes their assigned work and is about to go idle. Instead of sitting idle, the teammate is redirected to useful work that helps the overall team quality.

---

## Phase 2: Curriculum Generation

**When a designer finishes generating their MAP alternative:**

Redirect to: **Review another designer's alternative that hasn't been cross-reviewed yet**

Instructions to give the idle teammate:
```
Your alternative is complete. Now review one of the other designers' maps
that hasn't been reviewed yet. Read their MAP_ALT_{n}.md and provide
specific feedback via peer messaging:

1. What does their approach handle better than yours?
2. What does your approach handle better?
3. Are there any structural issues (sizing, sequencing, gaps)?
4. Would their approach work well for the audience in INTENT.md?

Be constructive but honest. The goal is to help the lead compile an
accurate COMPARISON.md.
```

---

## Phase 3: Curriculum Critique

**When a reviewer finishes their critique:**

Redirect to: **Review another reviewer's critique for blind spots**

Instructions to give the idle teammate:
```
Your critique is complete. Now read another reviewer's critique file
(critiques/CRITIQUE_{ROLE}.md) and look for blind spots from your
lens perspective:

1. Did they miss anything that your lens would catch?
2. Do you agree or disagree with their findings?
3. Are there findings from your critique that reinforce or contradict theirs?

Message the other reviewer directly to discuss any connections or
disagreements.
```

---

## Phase 5: Mission Building

**When a builder finishes all missions for their star:**

Redirect to: **Review completed missions from other stars for cross-star consistency**

Instructions to give the idle teammate:
```
Your star's missions are complete. Now review missions from other
completed stars and check for:

1. Terminology consistency: Are the same concepts called the same thing?
2. Running examples: Do cross-star references make sense?
3. Bridge accuracy: Do Bridge sections that reference your star correctly
   preview your content?
4. Difficulty progression: Does the overall difficulty flow make sense
   across star boundaries?

Message the relevant star's builder about any inconsistencies you find.
```

---

## Phase 6: Mission Critique

**When a reviewer finishes reviewing all missions:**

Redirect to: **Double-check highest-severity findings from other reviewers**

Instructions to give the idle teammate:
```
Your review is complete. Now look at other reviewers' CRITICAL findings
and verify them through your lens:

1. Read critical findings from other critiques/MISSION_CRITIQUE_*.md files
2. For each critical finding, check if your lens adds additional context
3. If you find connected issues, flag them as compound problems
4. If you disagree with a critical rating, message the reviewer to discuss

Focus only on CRITICAL findings — don't re-review everything.
```
